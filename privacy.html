Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how **X Lead Scraper** ("Website," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information from and about you when you visit our website at **xleadscraper.qzz.io** (the "Site").

1. Data We Collect

As a single-page marketing and informational website, we collect very limited data.

A. Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected):

When you visit our Site, certain non-personal data is collected automatically by our hosting provider (Netlify) or third-party analytics (if used, such as basic Google Analytics). This may include:

  • **Log Data:** Your IP address, browser type, internet service provider, time zone, referring/exit pages, and operating system.
  • **Usage Data:** Information about how you use the Site, such as the pages you view and the time spent on those pages.
  • **The Scarcity Counter:** The counter showing licenses remaining is handled solely by client-side JavaScript and does not transmit any personalized data to our servers.

B. Personal Information (Through Third Parties):

We do not directly collect or store sensitive personal information on this website. All sales and payment processing are handled by our trusted third-party e-commerce provider:

  • **Gumroad:** If you click a "Buy Now" button, you are redirected to **Gumroad**. Gumroad's collection and use of your name, email address, payment information, and transaction data is governed by the Gumroad Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information

We use the minimal collected information to:

  • **Analyze Performance:** Understand traffic patterns and measure the effectiveness of our marketing efforts.
  • **Improve the Site:** Ensure the Site loads quickly and functions correctly on all devices.
  • **Mitigate Risk:** Maintain proper legal compliance and transparency regarding the nature of our product (V2).

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We share information only in the following scenarios:

  • **With Gumroad:** Your purchase data is handled exclusively by Gumroad for transaction fulfillment.
  • **For Legal Reasons:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).

4. Third-Party Links

This Site contains links to third-party platforms, most notably **Gumroad** (for purchasing) and **Swapd** (for story verification). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any site you visit.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding the personal information we hold about you, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. As we only hold non-personal usage data, these requests should primarily be directed to **Gumroad** regarding your purchase data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
